# Data manifest
- **Owner:** Content & balance team
- **Labels in use:** `prototype` for experimental items, `shipping` for finalized data, `deprecated` for retirements, `test-only` for fixtures.
- **Retention:** `deprecated` assets older than 30 days should be reviewed for removal or archival; the asset auditor will flag them automatically.
- **Notes:** Keep item assets labeled to clarify lifecycle and prevent untracked additions.
